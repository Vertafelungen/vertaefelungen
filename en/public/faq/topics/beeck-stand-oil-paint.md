# FAQ: Beeck Stand Oil Interior Paint Pro

## 1. What is Beeck Stand Oil Interior Paint Pro and what is it used for?

Beeck Stand Oil Interior Paint Pro is a highly pigmented linseed stand oil paint in **professional quality** for interiors – ideal for doors, furniture, wall and ceiling panelling. It cures by oxidative drying to form a **breathable**, water-resistant oil-based finish with a satin or semi-gloss sheen ([beeck.com](https://www.beeck.com/en/products/bmf-product.php?p=interior-stand-oil-paint-pro-new-2019)).

---

## 2. Why is Beeck considered a premium, traditional wood protection brand?

Beeck has stood for natural, high-performance coatings since 1894. The products are valued by professionals in painting, furniture making and restoration for being heritage-compliant, trade-quality and thoroughly tested. Beeck remains committed to plant-based formulations without synthetic resins – proven in heritage conservation and modern restoration ([beeck.com](https://www.beeck.com/en/products/bmf-product.php?p=interior-stand-oil-paint-pro-new-2019)).

## 3. Who is Stand Oil Interior Paint Pro aimed at?

This paint is aimed at discerning users such as restorers, craftsmen and painting contractors who value **premium, sustainable quality, heritage-appropriate processing** and lasting colour results.

## 4. What colour options are available?

Beeck Stand Oil Interior Paint Pro is available as **full-tone and mixed colours** based on the Beeck colour card. It can also be mixed to **RAL, NCS** or special samples (e.g. Farrow & Ball) for maximum colour precision ([beeck.com](https://www.beeck.com/en/products/bmf-product.php?p=interior-stand-oil-paint-pro-new-2019)).

---

## 5. What are the key properties of the paint?

- **Sheen:** satin or semi-gloss  
- **Solids content:** >70% (high-solid), very economical (approx. 0.09 L/m² per coat)  
- **sd-value:** approx. 0.50 m – breathable and water-resistant  
- Excellent adhesion, low-tension, overcoatable layers – ideal for heritage use ([beeck.com](https://www.beeck.com/en/products/bmf-product.php?p=interior-stand-oil-paint-pro-new-2019))

---

## 6. What does the paint smell like during application?

The characteristic scent is mild, oily and slightly nutty – typical of linseed stand oil paints. It dissipates rapidly after drying and is not obtrusive. This natural scent profile is distinct from synthetic varnishes.

---

## 7. Brief application guidance

1. Prepare the substrate: clean, dry and sound  
2. Prime e.g. with Beeck Wood Primer or Undercoat Pro  
3. Apply two thin coats, allowing at least 24 hrs drying between coats  
4. Allow to dry in a well-ventilated, shaded space  
5. The paint can be recoated indefinitely without having to remove old coats ([beeck.com](https://www.beeck.com/en/products/bmf-product.php?p=interior-primer))
