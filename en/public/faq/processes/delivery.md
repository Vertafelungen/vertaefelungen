# FAQ: Delivery
## 1. When is delivery scheduled?
- Delivery takes place **after receipt of the final payment**, once manufacturing is complete.

- You will be informed in advance and receive a delivery date with the carrier.

***

## 2. How is shipping organised & what is included?
- The panelling is shipped **in wooden crates by freight carrier**.

- Packages up to approx. 240 cm are unloaded **kerbside**; larger packages are delivered **to the truck**.

***

## 3. In what condition is the wood delivered?
- All elements are **planed, sanded and primed**, with a residual moisture of approx. 12%.

- The wood should **rest in the room for one week** before installation to acclimatise.

- Fixed elements such as panels are already assembled; long mouldings are supplied separately and may have excess length for onsite adjustment.

***

## 4. How does installation work?
- On request, we offer **installation up to the final brushstroke**; the final invoice is issued afterwards.

- Alternatively, installation is carried out by you or your chosen specialist.

***

## 5. What should be considered at delivery?
- Inspect the goods **upon handover** in the presence of the driver for visible damage and correct number of packages. Note any damage on the delivery note and send photos by e-mail.

- The goods are **insured against transport damage**, insurance is included in the delivery price.

***
