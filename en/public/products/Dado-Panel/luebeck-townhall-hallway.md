# Lübeck – Town Hall – Long Hallway (P0022)

A paneled wainscot with a classic rectangular frame structure from the historic hallway of Lübeck’s Town Hall.

The entire length of the “Langes Haus” features this elegant wall panelling with solid oak profiles and pinewood insets. Its timeless, rectangular layout suits Neo-Gothic and Hanseatic-style interiors.

Presumably built during the 1893 renovations.
