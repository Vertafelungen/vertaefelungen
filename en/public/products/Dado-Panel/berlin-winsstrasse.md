# Berlin – Winsstraße (P0001)

A classic example of Art Nouveau panelling found in historic residential areas of Berlin.  
The Wins quarter in Prenzlauer Berg is characterised by **high-quality stucco buildings**,  
street cafés, book and wine shops — and finely detailed wood panelling like this one.

This model features an **original height of 110 cm** and elongated, horizontal **picture-frame mouldings**.  
It is ideal for custom room dimensions and available in **oak or pine**, with optional preparation for painting.

Made to order – delivery time approx. **6 weeks**, plus shipping.
