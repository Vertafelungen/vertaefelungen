## Description

This turpentine oil is gently distilled from pine resin and serves as a natural thinner for oils, varnishes, and resin mixtures. It evaporates cleanly and has a pleasant scent.

## Application

- For thinning linseed oil, tung oil, and oil varnishes  
- Making resin-oil mixes (e.g., with rosin or dammar)  
- Cleaning brushes and dissolving oils during restoration  
- **Not suitable for food contact**

## Technical Notes

- Evaporates without residue  
- Biogenic and solvent-free (in strict definition)  
- Oily cloths may self-ignite – dry under supervision
