# Berlin – Sophienstraße (P0004)

Sophienstraße in Berlin is known for its charming, small-town character and for having the **only surviving baroque church tower** in the city.  
From here, you can walk to the Hackesche Höfe and the Sophien-Gips courtyards.

This panel features a **vertically oriented coffer**, which can be easily adapted to suit individual room dimensions.  
We manufacture the wainscoting **true to the original** in either **oak or pine**.  
Upon request, the surface can be **prepared for painting**.

Made to order – **approx. 6 weeks**, plus shipping.
