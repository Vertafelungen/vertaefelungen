# Rome – St. Peter’s Basilica – Balustrade at the Tomb of St. Peter (P0010)

This paneling is based on the historic balustrade surrounding the staircase to the Vatican Necropolis in the very heart of St. Peter’s Basilica in Rome – directly in front of Bernini’s Baldachin, above the tomb of the Apostle Peter.

Its **shape, proportions, and construction** follow the original faithfully. The varied colour tones of marble can be replicated by using different types of wood.
