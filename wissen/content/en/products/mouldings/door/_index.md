Door surrounds frame doors and openings and strongly influence the perceived proportions of doors within a room. Depending on style, they can be restrained, profile-heavy or more sculptural.\n\n**Typical applications:** interior doors, apartment doors, openings and portal-style frames.\n\nChoose a profile to view details, dimensions, variants and images.

## Quick answer

<!-- ssot_texts | v2026-02-28 09:55 Europe/Berlin | source: categories.csv:body_en_kurzantwort | key:door-architraves | path_de:produkte/leisten/tuerbekleidungen | path_en:products/mouldings/door -->

- Door architraves frame openings and strongly influence room character.
- Profile and width should align with skirting and wall moulding language.
- In existing buildings, wall thickness, reveal depth, and height references are critical.
- Early detailing reduces rework at miters and transitions.

## Practical context

<!-- ssot_texts | v2026-02-28 09:55 Europe/Berlin | source: categories.csv:body_en_praxis | key:door-architraves | path_de:produkte/leisten/tuerbekleidungen | path_en:products/mouldings/door -->

In practice, architraves often act as the lead detail for the whole moulding family. Once proportions work at the door, skirting and wall mouldings can be coordinated consistently.

## Decisions & variants

<!-- ssot_texts | v2026-02-28 09:55 Europe/Berlin | source: categories.csv:body_en_varianten | key:door-architraves | path_de:produkte/leisten/tuerbekleidungen | path_en:products/mouldings/door -->

Variants range from restrained linear profiles to richer historic articulations. Solutions also differ for single-sided vs double-sided casing and different reveal depths.

## Process & planning

<!-- ssot_texts | v2026-02-28 09:55 Europe/Berlin | source: categories.csv:body_en_ablauf | key:door-architraves | path_de:produkte/leisten/tuerbekleidungen | path_en:products/mouldings/door -->

Process: measure opening, select profile and casing width, resolve skirting transitions, manufacture, install, and align final finish. Clean visible miters are essential.

## Cost logic

<!-- ssot_texts | v2026-02-28 09:55 Europe/Berlin | source: categories.csv:body_en_kosten | key:door-architraves | path_de:produkte/leisten/tuerbekleidungen | path_en:products/mouldings/door -->

Cost drivers include number of openings, profile complexity, reveal conditions, required adjustments, and finish quality. Complex transition details increase labor.

## Common mistakes & how to avoid them

<!-- ssot_texts | v2026-02-28 09:55 Europe/Berlin | source: categories.csv:body_en_fehler | key:door-architraves | path_de:produkte/leisten/tuerbekleidungen | path_en:products/mouldings/door -->

Frequent mistakes are poor proportions, inconsistent height logic, and weak transitions to skirting. A coordinated detail set with reference heights prevents these issues.
